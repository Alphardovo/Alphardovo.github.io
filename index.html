Introduction of Project:

Football is a sport that many people enjoy. The Premier League, Ligue 1, Bundesliga, Serie A and La Liga in football have been around for hundreds of years. In this project, we want to analysis the performance of football players(path1) and teams(path2) from 2021 to 2022 and then do the prediction for future performance. Cause only one year data is not enough to prove the future prediction. We also use the player data from 2017 to 2020(path3 to path5). We wanted to know what factors influence football players' performance on the field, and what factors influence the number of wins for a football team. Through such data analysis, it is possible to predict the development of players or teams in the future. For example, if a team's players are all older, the team's probability of winning the game will decrease.
