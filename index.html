Introduction of Project:

Football is a sport that many people enjoy. The Premier League, Ligue 1, Bundesliga, Serie A and La Liga in football have been around for hundreds of years. In this project, we want to analysis the performance of football players and teams from 2021 to 2022 and then do the prediction for future performance. We wanted to know what factors influence football players' performance on the field, and what factors influence the number of wins for a football team. Through such data analysis, it is possible to predict the development of players or teams in the future. For example, if a team's players are all older, the team's probability of winning the game will decrease.

Simple Timeline for Project

Our team will have a meeting on Fridays every week to discuss each other's completion, problems encountered and solutions. Maria Chen will be mainly responsible for the analysis of Player in the project, and Haochen Chen will be mainly responsible for the analysis of Team in the project. After both parties have completed the analysis of their respective databases, we will integrate the data from our analysis and finally work together to predict the future performance of players and teams.

Data: 2021-2022 Football Team Stats¶

This part of datasource is mainly focused on the soccer teams' statistics. The reason we chose this data is because this table details the statistics of football teams from 2021 to 2022. The tabular data includes most teams in the Premier League (from England,ENG), Ligue 1 (from France,FRA), Bundesliga (from German,GER), Serie A (from Italy,ITA), and La Liga (from Spain,ESP). The table contains various details for each team: ranking, team name, matches played, total field goals for the season, points per game, attendance per game during the season, etc. We wanted to analyze this data to find out what factors influenced each team's performance.
